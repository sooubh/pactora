How to Use Pactora

A simple guide based on the documented app flow.

1. Get started

  • Open Pactora to the splash screen while the local database initializes.
  • Complete the five-step onboarding walkthrough (Privacy, Promises, Items, Money).
  • Grant notification access if you want reminder alerts.

2. Add people

Use the People list to add the contacts tied to your commitments. Each person gets a full relationship history.

3. Log a commitment

  • Tap the floating action button to open the quick add sheet.
  • Choose Promises, Borrow & Lend, or Money based on what you want to track.
  • Set due dates, priorities, and attachments like photos when needed.

4. Monitor your dashboard

  • Use the Home dashboard to see Pending, Overdue, Borrowed, and Owed summaries.
  • Review “Due Today” items to take immediate action.
  • Open detail screens to complete, snooze, or share entries.

5. Search and review history

  • Use Global Search to find any promise, item, or IOU instantly.
  • Open the Activity Timeline for a chronological history of your actions.
  • Use the Calendar view to scan commitments by date.

6. Protect your data

  • Go to Settings > Data & Backup to export a JSON backup.
  • Import the backup on another device if you need to migrate data.

7. Optional upgrades

Pactora Plus removes ads and unlocks custom themes, analytics, and additional notification options.

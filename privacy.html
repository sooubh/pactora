Summary

Pactora is built with privacy-by-design principles. The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to a server.

Data collection
  • No account creation, login, or cloud sync is used.
  • No personal data is sent to Pactora’s servers because none exist for storage.
Data stored on your device
  • Commitments, people, and IOUs are stored locally in an Isar database.
  • Item photos and attachments are stored as local files on your device.
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently.
Permissions
  • Notifications: Used to schedule local reminders for promises and return dates.
  • Media/Photos: Used to attach item photos. Files stay on your device.
Backups
  • Manual backup exports are available via Settings > Data & Backup.
  • Backups are exported as JSON files that you control and store.
Advertising and third parties
  • Pactora uses Google AdMob banner ads in the free version.
  • Google may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads unless you opt out.
  • Ads are banner-only and do not interrupt the app flow.
Children’s privacy

Pactora is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to this policy

Updates will be reflected in the app’s About & FAQ screens. No update cadence is specified in the project files.

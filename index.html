How Pactora works

A simple flow designed for fast entry and clear accountability.

See full guide
  1. Complete the onboarding walkthrough and allow notifications for reminders.
  2. Add people you interact with in the People list.
  3. Use the quick add button to log a promise, borrowed item, or IOU.
  4. Track statuses (pending, overdue, done) and review the activity timeline.
  5. Back up your data anytime from Settings > Data & Backup.

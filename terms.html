Acceptable use

Pactora is designed for informal tracking of promises, borrowed items, and small IOUs. Users are responsible for the accuracy of the data they enter.

Not a legal record

Pactora should not be treated as a legally binding record or financial ledger.

Offline data responsibility
  • All information is stored locally on your device.
  • Deleting the app permanently deletes all data.
  • Use the manual backup feature to protect your records.
Ads and premium upgrade
  • The free version displays banner ads via Google AdMob.
  • Pactora Plus is a one-time upgrade that removes ads and unlocks premium features.
Limitation of liability

Pactora is not liable for data loss caused by device failure, OS updates, or app deletion.

Eligibility

Pactora is not intended for children under 13.

Governing law

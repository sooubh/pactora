# 10. FAQ & Support — Pactora

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Your data is stored in a local, encrypted database (Isar) on your phone. It never leaves your device.

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Data & Backup and tap "Export Backup". This will save a JSON file which you can store in your personal cloud or on another device.

**Q: Can I sync data between my iPhone and Android?**
A: Not automatically. You can manually export the backup from one and import it on the other.

**Q: What happens if I delete the app?**
A: ALL data will be permanently lost. We do not have a copy of your data. Always keep a manual backup.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**
A: Yes, 100%. Internet is only required to load ads in the free version. Core features are fully functional offline.

**Q: How do I remove ads?**
A: Tap the "Pro" or "Premium" icon in Settings and purchase "Pactora Plus".

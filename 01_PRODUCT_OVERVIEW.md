# 01. Product Overview — Pactora

## Core Value Proposition
Pactora is a high-performance, privacy-centric mobile application designed for individuals who value reliability and personal accountability. It serves as a **100% Offline Commitment Tracker**, allowing users to record, monitor, and fulfill promises, manage borrowed or lent items, and track informal financial IOUs without any cloud synchronization or account requirements.

## The Problem
In today's fast-paced world, small informal commitments—"I'll return your book," "I'll pay for the tea," "I'll call you back tonight"—often get lost in the noise. Existing tools are either too complex (project management software), too rigid (calendars), or too invasive (cloud-synced note apps). Pactora addresses the "trust friction" caused by these forgotten micro-commitments.

## The Solution: Relational Memory
Pactora is optimized for the **relational** nature of commitments. Unlike a standard Todo list, every entry in Pactora is linked to a **Person**. This creates a "Trust Profile" over time, helping users identify their own patterns and maintain better relationships through consistency.

## Target Audience
- **Students:** Tracking shared resources (chargers, books, notes) and small split expenses.
- **Freelancers:** Managing informal verbal agreements with multiple clients.
- **Privacy Advocates:** Users who refuse to put personal relationship data on a central server.
- **Community Leaders:** People managing informal group activities who need to track delegated tasks.

## Key Differentiators
1. **100% Offline:** No login, no server, no internet required. Data sovereignty is absolute.
2. **Specialized Domains:** Dedicated logic for Promises, Borrow/Lend, and Money.
3. **Relation-Centric:** Everything revolves around people, not just tasks.
4. **Lightweight & Fast:** Built with Flutter and Isar for sub-millisecond database queries.

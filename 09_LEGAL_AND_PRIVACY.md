# 09. Legal & Privacy — Pactora

## Privacy Policy (Summary)
Pactora is designed with **Privacy by Design** principles. 
- **Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from the app to our servers.
- **Device Access:** We request access to Notifications (for reminders) and Media (for item photos). This data remains on your device.
- **Third Parties:** We use Google AdMob for advertising. Google may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads unless opted out.
- **Data Deletion:** Deleting the app permanently deletes all stored data.

## Terms of Service (Summary)
- **Usage:** Users are responsible for the data they enter. Pactora is not a legal record and should be used for informal tracking only.
- **Liability:** We are not liable for any data loss caused by hardware failure, OS updates, or app deletion.
- **Governing Law:** This app is developed and maintained in Maharashtra, India.

## Compliance
- **GDPR:** Compliant by virtue of zero data collection.
- **COPPA:** Not intended for children under 13.
- **DPDP (India):** Compliant; user has full control over local data.

# 08. Marketing & ASO — Pactora

## Play Store Metadata

**App Title:** Pactora: Offline Promise Tracker
**Short Description:** Never forget a promise. 100% private and offline commitment & IOU tracker.

**Full Description:**
Pactora is your ultimate personal accountability partner. Designed for those who value their word, Pactora helps you track every promise, borrowed item, and informal debt in one beautiful, private, and 100% offline app.

**Key Highlights:**
- 🔒 **100% Private:** No cloud, no accounts, no internet required. Your data is yours.
- 📋 **Promises:** Track commitments made to you and by you.
- 📦 **Borrow & Lend:** Manage physical items with photos and return dates.
- 💰 **Money Tracker:** Informal IOU management with partial payment tracking.
- 👤 **People Centric:** See your full relationship history with every contact.

**Search Keywords:**
promise tracker, borrow lend tracker, iou tracker, money manager, debt tracker, personal CRM, accountability app, offline notes, privacy tool, relationship manager, bill split tracker, lend tracker, item tracker, task reminders, private diary.

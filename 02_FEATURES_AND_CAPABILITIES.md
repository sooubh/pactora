# 02. Features & Capabilities — Pactora

## 1. Promises Module
Track verbal or written commitments made between you and others.
- **Bi-directional Tracking:** Toggle between "I promised" and "They promised me."
- **Categorization:** Tag promises as Task, Meeting, Payment, Callback, or Errand.
- **Priority Levels:** Low, Medium, and High with visual indicators.
- **Smart Reminders:** Local notifications scheduled at custom offsets (e.g., 1 hour before).
- **Completion States:** Transition from Pending to Done, Overdue, or Cancelled.

## 2. Borrow & Lend Module
A specialized inventory tracker for physical items.
- **Item History:** Record when an item was handed over and its condition.
- **Photo Proof:** Attach local images of the item to avoid disputes over condition.
- **Estimated Value:** Track the monetary value of items for insurance/replacement reference.
- **Return Deadlines:** Specific dates for expected returns with overdue alerts.

## 3. Informal Money Records
Manage non-bank IOUs and small cash transactions.
- **Transaction Direction:** Clear visual distinction between "I Owe" and "They Owe Me."
- **Partial Payments:** Add multiple payments to a single record with a progress bar.
- **Currency Support:** Manual entry for various currency symbols (defaulting to local).
- **Settlement Logic:** Mark as fully settled while retaining history.

## 4. People Management
A localized CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system.
- **Consolidated View:** See all promises, items, and money linked to a specific person.
- **Member History:** Track when a person was first added to your circle.
- **Trust Indicators:** (Roadmap) Visual cues showing the percentage of promises kept.

## 5. System Features
- **Global Search:** Full-text search across all text fields in all modules.
- **Activity Timeline:** A chronological feed of every action taken in the app.
- **Manual Backups:** Export and Import your entire database as a JSON file.
- **Theme Engine:** Adaptive Material 3 design with Dark and Light modes.
- **Monetization (Plus):** One-time upgrade to remove ads and unlock "Nag Mode" (repeat notifications).
